The Mission

Building a Future of Engineers

Robotics involves the design, construction, programming, and operation of robots. This is often done as part of a robotics club or team, and can involve participating in competitions such as FIRST Robotics or VEX Robotics. Moses Brown currently competes in VEX Robotics.

This Year's VEX Robotics Game: High Stakes

The 2024-2025 VEX Robotics VRC game, "High Stakes," challenges teams to score points by placing rings on stakes, positioning mobile goals, and climbing a central ladder. Click for more information.

Meet the Team

The 2024-2025 Moses Brown VEX Robotics Team consists of 3 separate teams. Team 1784X consists of seniors, 1784Y consists of juniors, and 1784Z consists of 9th graders. Click for more information.

Explore the Home of Moses Brown Robotics: The Y-Lab
