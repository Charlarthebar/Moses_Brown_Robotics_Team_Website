The Mission

Building a Future of Engineers

Robotics involves the design, construction, programming, and operation of robots. This is often done as part of a robotics club or team, and can involve participating in competitions such as FIRST Robotics or VEX Robotics. Moses Brown currently competes in VEX Robotics.

Explore the Moses Brown Robotics Program

This Year's VEX Robotics Game: High Stakes

The 2024-2025 VEX Robotics VRC game, "High Stakes," challenges teams to score points by placing rings on stakes, positioning mobile goals, and climbing a central ladder. The game is divided into a 15-second autonomous period and a 1:45 driver-controlled period, with additional points awarded for scoring during the autonomous phase. Strategic goal placement and robot climbing height can earn alliances significant points. Click for more information.

Meet the Team

The 2024-2025 Moses Brown VEX Robotics Team Consists of 3 separate teams. Team 1784X consists of seniors, 1784Y consists of juniors, and 1784Z consists of 9th graders. Though the older teams have more experience, each Moses Brown Robotics team is made up of bright, talented, smart, and passionate young engineers. Click for more information.

Explore the Home of Moses Brown Robotics: The Y-Lab

The Moses Brown School Y-Lab is an innovative makerspace designed to foster creativity and hands-on learning. It provides students with access to tools such as 3D printers, laser cutters, and woodworking equipment, encouraging them to explore engineering, design, and technology projects. The Y-Lab supports interdisciplinary collaboration, allowing students to bring their ideas to life through experimentation and practical problem-solving. Click for more information.
